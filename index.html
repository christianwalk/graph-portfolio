About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research explores hands-on techniques for integrating large language models and other generative tools into professional and academic workflows. Participants learn prompt engineering, data-aware generation, multimodal approaches, ethical considerations, and deployment strategies.

The eight-week curriculum is project-based, culminating in a capstone that showcases how generative AI can address real-world challenges in data analysis, reporting, and communication.
